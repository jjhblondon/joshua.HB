STUFFED

Environment Artist & Redshift Sets

← Back to Gallery

I worked as one of two environment artists for the animated teaser of a potential Stuffed series. We worked closely with the directors to design and quickly build whimsical sets that captured the magical essence of the Stuffed world. Through collaboration, we helped ensure the environments were in line with the tone and vision of the series, translating the playful and fantastical elements of the comic into animated spaces.

Working on limited time, we had some concepts and a rough animatic that we followed while creating these 4 sets. These sets were all created in Redshift—my first time working in Redshift in Maya. Learning to make a modular and optimized Redshift environment was a learning experience in itself, but the excitement of the team around me filled me with motivation to produce the best work I could.
