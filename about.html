A People-First Approach

Hi, I’m Josh, and I’ve always been obsessed with the 'how' behind things. My work lives at that intersection of how do we get from an idea through to a finished product. I act as a technical bridge to ensure the people behind the tech can do their best work. From real-time rendering in Unreal Engine to complex data ingestion, I believe technology is at its best when it's making an incredible story possible.

My journey has always been rooted in collaboration, which has shaped my people-first approach to work. I believe that great ideas are born from diverse perspectives and strong teamwork. This mindset has naturally led me into a technical project manager role, where I can further nurture creativity and problem-solving while ensuring that technical teams thrive.
